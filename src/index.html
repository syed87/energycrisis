Has your Energy supplier gone bust?

Surging gas prices have left the energy industry in crisis. Price increases are due to an increase in demand for gas as we head into winter and the economy emerges from the pandemic. Scroll down to find out if your energy supplier has gone bust and what steps you can take next.

Find a new Energy Supplier
1. Take a meter reading

Remember to take a meter reading so you can submit this to your new supplier once Ofgen notifies you which company you've been assigned to.

2. Sit tight

Your new supplier will get in touch, any attempt to switch at this stage may be blocked.

3. Search for a new supplier

Scan the market for a deal worth switching to. The good news is that you will not be charged any exit fees to switch.
